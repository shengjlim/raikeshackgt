unmixr.

Take the drums, vocals, or everything else out of a song.

  • Karaoke Track

  • A Cappella Track

  • Drum Track

To use:

  1. Paste a Youtube URL from the song you'd like to modify
  2. Select desired mode
  3. Click Submit and wait for the file to generate
  4. Play the Youtube video, your new audio will play along!
  5. Download the audio if you wish

Enjoy your newly unmixed tracks.
